## Why Grinders Matter More Than Machines

> *"Buy the best grinder you can afford before upgrading your machine."*
> вҖ” Near-universal specialty coffee consensus

Grind quality determines:
- **Particle size uniformity** вҶ’ extraction consistency
- **Grind distribution** вҶ’ cup clarity and character
- **Heat generation** вҶ’ flavour preservation
- **Retention** вҶ’ freshness of each dose
- **Repeatability** вҶ’ shot-to-shot consistency

A mediocre grinder on a great machine produces mediocre espresso. A great grinder on a mediocre machine produces surprisingly good espresso.

### Conical Burrs

```

         в”Ңв”Җв”Җв”җ

```

| Attribute | Conical Burr |
|---------|--------------|
| Particle distribution | Bimodal (fines + coarses) |
| Flavor profile | Body, sweetness, roundness; complex layering |
| Heat generation | Lower (gravity-assisted; less friction) |
| Retention | Lower (gravity clears grounds from burrs) |
| Best for | Espresso body and sweetness; single-dose |
| Examples | Niche Zero, Lido, Comandante (hand), Kinu (hand), Ek43S conical |

## Single-Dose vs On-Demand (Hopper)

| Design | Single-Dose | On-Demand (Hopper) |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| **Usage** | Weigh and grind per dose | Pre-filled hopper; dose by time or count |
| **Retention** | Near-zero | 0.5вҖ“5g+ retained in grinder |
| **Freshness** | Maximum вҖ” grind only what you use | Risk of stale retention in hopper |
| **Workflow** | Slower (weigh each time) | Faster (pre-set dose) |
| **Best for** | Home; specialty single-origin; experimentation | High-volume cafГ© service |
| **Examples** | Niche Zero, DF64, Lagom P64 | MahlkГ¶nig E65S, Mazzer Major, Eureka Mignon |

## RDT (Ross Droplet Technique)

Add **1вҖ“2 drops of water** to beans before grinding:
- Reduces static electricity
- Prevents grounds clumping and sticking to grinder chute
- Improves dose accuracy
- Does not measurably affect cup quality at this volume

Simple, free, highly effective вҖ” used by most home baristas.
